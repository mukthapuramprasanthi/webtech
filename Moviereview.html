Movie Review

Nannaku Prematho

Audience Rating Summary

4.5/5. IMDb

Release date : January 13 2016

Director : Sukumar
Producer : B. V. S. N. Prasad
Music Director : Devi Sri Prasad
Starring : NTR, Rakul Preet Singh,Rajendra Prasad, Jagapati Babu

Story:

story is set in London. Subramanyam alias Ramesh Chandra Prasad (Rajendra Prasad) is cheated by an evil entrepreneur Krishnamoorthy (Jagapathi Babu). Years pass, his three sons grow up to be successful in life. But Subramanyam has only one dream – to see the fall of Krishnamoorthy. His younger son Abhiram decides to fulfill his father’s wish by bringing down the business empire of Krishnamoorthy.
